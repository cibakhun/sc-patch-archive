CONTESTED ZONES

THE OBJECTIVE: 7 COMBOARDS

🏆
THE GOAL: Collect all 7 Unique Comboards scattered across three major stations to unlock exclusive ship rewards (Corsair, F8C, etc.)
Warning: Extreme PvPvE zone. Prepare for heavy combat.
COMBOARD LOCATIONS

STATION OVERVIEWS
Checkmate Station
Multiple entry points via elevators. High ambush risk.

Orbituary Station
Linear design. Dangerous progression choke points specifically behind Red Keycard doors.
FUSE DOOR MECHANICS

Some doors require functional Fuses to operate. Bring Multi-Tools/Tractor beams to manually insert them if prompts fail.

FUSE
Required for dead doors. Can occasionally become "bricked" by other players.

OUTPOST ACTIVATION

ASTEROID OUTPOSTS PIPELINE

To actually use the Comboards, you must extract them at active outposts. They operate on a long-duration timer cycle.

INSERT COMBOARDS

PvPvE STRATEGY
COMBAT READINESS
  • Audio is critical: Rely on footsteps to hear approaching players attempting to steal comboards.
  • Control the Chokes: Entrances to keycard printer locations and supervisor outposts have massive player traffic.
  • Bring Escorts: Because 24-minute timer defends are required at Asteroid Outposts, going solo is extremely dangerous.
